No-Bake Oreo Cheesecake Recipe

Description

A no-bake Oreo cheesecake is a chilled dessert with a creamy, tangy cheesecake filling set in the fridge (not the oven), usually on top of an Oreo cookie crust. What it’s like:Crust: crushed Oreos mixed with melted butter, pressed into a pan—firm, chocolatey, and slightly crunchy. Filling: smooth and fluffy cream-cheese mixture (often cream cheese + powdered sugar + vanilla), lightened with whipped cream or whipped topping. It’s rich but airy, with that classic cheesecake tang. Oreo factor: chopped Oreos folded into the filling, plus extra crushed cookies or whole Oreos on top for texture. Texture: creamy and mousse-like when cold, slices cleanly once fully chilled; softer than baked cheesecake. Flavor: sweet vanilla cheesecake balanced by cocoa cookie crumbs and a mild chocolate note. Common variations include a chocolate drizzle, extra Oreo layers inside, or mini individual cups instead of a full cake.

History

No-bake Oreo cheesecake is a late-20th-century/modern crowd-pleaser built from mid-century no-bake cheesecake techniques and icebox-dessert traditions, using Oreos (a 1912 classic) as the crust and mix-in.

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 (18 ounce) package Oreo Cookies, divided
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 4 (8 ounce) packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 (8 ounce) tub Cool Whip Whipped Topping, thawed

Direcrtions-Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. To make the crust: Set aside 15 cookies for the filling. Finely crush remaining cookies in a large bowl.
  3. Add melted butter and mix to combine.
  4. Press buttery crumbs firmly into the bottom of a 9x13-inch pan. Place in the refrigerator.
  5. To make the filling: Coarsely chop the reserved cookies.
  6. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla in large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until well blended.
  7. Gently stir in whipped topping and chopped cookies.
  8. Spread over crust. Cover and refrigerate until firm, about 4 hours. Cut into 20 squares.
